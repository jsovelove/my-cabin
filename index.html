My Cabin in the Woods

At the beginning of the covid pandemic, no one was sure what was in store and how long we would be stuck in isolation.
When the world started shutting down, My girlfriend Thea and I fled to my family's house on Lopez Island to wait out what we thought would just be a couple of weeks of chaos on the mainland.
Days passed.....weeks passed.... and a few months later reality had set in and I realized I was going to be waiting out the pandemic indefinetly.

We had hardly left the property during that period, relying on our garden and deliverys for food. I was going crazy from the absense of structure in my life.
As a way to keep myself entertained, I downloaded some software called SketchUp and started designing a small cabin.
