The framing process was a ton of fun.
I was able to source a lot of the framing materials for free from a friend who was demoing an old skatepark he had built.
But unfortunatly progress came to a hault because I ran out of money to continue.
I had decided to try and build a cabin when building materials were at an all time high.
So I decided to use the skills I had been gaining and get a job doing carpentry while I waited for lumber prices to drop back down.
Once I got a few paychecks, I continued the project.
