The Dutch Door

After getting the cabin all dried in. I put the project on hold to go back to school.
When summer came back around, I picked the project back up and started working on the doors and windows.
Someone I worked for over the summer was nice enough to give me this old door, which I decided to cut in half.

The Windows

Thea's parents had some old single pane window sashes from their house which they let me use in the cabin. Because they are just sashes, I had to build the casing for each window. I'm still currently working on this project.

During this time, we started setting the inside of the cabin up for summer living.
